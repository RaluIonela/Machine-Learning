1. MODEL PERFORMANCE METRICS
-----------------------------------------------------------

| Model               | PR-AUC | ROC-AUC | F1-Score | Brier | ECE  |

| Logistic Regression | 0.26   | 0.66    | 0.36     | 0.25  | 0.09 |

- The **LSTM** demonstrates superior discrimination (highest PR-AUC and ROC-AUC)
  and the best calibration (lowest Brier and ECE).

-----------------------------------------------------------
2. FEATURE GROUP ABLATION ANALYSIS
-----------------------------------------------------------

The LSTM model was retrained after systematically removing
specific feature groups to determine their relative contribution.

| Removed Feature Group | ΔPR-AUC | Interpretation |
|------------------------|---------|----------------|
| Music features         | -0.17   | Most influential predictors of overstimulation risk |
| Noise features         | -0.10   | Moderate influence (environmental factor) |
| Context/time features  | -0.04   | Minor contribution to short-term prediction |

3. RECOMMENDATION MODULE RESULTS
-----------------------------------------------------------

1. calm_piano  (Predicted risk: 0.12)

3. lo_fi_focus (Predicted risk: 0.19)

Observation:
The recommender consistently prioritizes low-arousal, mid-tempo tracks,
aligning with prior findings in affective computing (Carvalho et al., 2022;
Su, Chang and Chen, 2020).
